Our Story

At Castillo Coffee, we're passionate about every step of the coffee journey. From carefully selecting the finest beans to roasting them to perfection in-house, we ensure every cup tells a story.

Our cozy shop is designed to be a haven for coffee lovers - a place to savor expertly crafted drinks or take home our freshly roasted beans to enjoy.

Every batch is roasted with care, ground fresh, and brewed to highlight the unique characteristics of each origin.
